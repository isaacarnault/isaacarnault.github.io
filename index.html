    Bloowatch is a web app intended to list and describe endangered species. It lets anybody create endangered species and environment-related alerts, and stay up to date with the news in that area. Our goal is to display live alerts on a world map which will enable visitors to see at a glance all most pressing endangered species issues around the globe.
