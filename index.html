    Doctomax

    Doctomax© is a news platform bootstrapped in 2020 for healthcare professionals, hospital and clinic directors. It provides live news, a knowledge base, job guidance and a focus on healthcare management systems and tools.

    MAR 2019

    Co-Founder & Chief Data Officer

    Bloowatch

    Bloowatch is a web app intended to list and describe Earth's endangered species. It lets anybody create endangered species and environment-related alerts, and stay up to date with the news in that area. Our goal is to display live alerts on a world map which will enable visitors to see at a glance all most pressing endangered species issues around the globe. Bloowatch's vision is to spread the knowledge and awareness about endangered species as much as possible, so we also created an API which lets other sites to benefit from our knowledge base.

    AUG 2017

    Co-Founder & Chief Technical Officer

    BitcoinToro

    BitcoinToro is intended to demystify the access to Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies by offering a knowledge base, daily news, live insights and metrics related to Bitcoin and other useful content. The product is one of those you can trust in the blockchain ecosystem. Today, great corporations such as eToro advertise with us. Product funded on sole assets.
